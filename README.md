# What is Condemned Moderation?

Welcome! This guide will teach you how to use and operate the bot with your current permissions and also tell you what else the bot is capable of.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Please note that the bot is currently private and cannot be invited to other servers. This may change in the future.**
{% endhint %}

The bot's prefix is **c;**

\*\*\*\*

From moderation commands, to announcing important things, to verifying users, and even adding a role to members with a simple click of a reaction, Condemned Moderation is capable of many things.
